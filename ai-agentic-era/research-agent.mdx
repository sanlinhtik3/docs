Research Agent ရဲ့တာဝန်က “အဖြေထုတ်ပေးတာ” မဟုတ်ပါ။ **Source တွေကိုစစ်၊ evidence ခွဲ၊ uncertainty ပြ၊ decision support ပေးတာ** ပါ။

## TL;DR

- AI research output ကို source မရှိရင် final truth မယူပါနဲ့။
- Summary ထက် evidence table ပိုတန်ဖိုးရှိပါတယ်။
- Missing information ကို AI ကဖုံးကွယ်မထားဘဲဖော်ပြရမယ်။
- Research முடிந்தရင် decision မဟုတ်ဘဲ next questions ပေးရမယ်။

## Research Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Question">
    Research question ကိုတိတိကျကျရေးပါ။
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sources">
    Official docs, primary sources, credible reports, original posts ကိုစုပါ။
  </Step>
  <Step title="Extract">
    Key claims, evidence, dates, caveats ကိုခွဲထုတ်ပါ။
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compare">
    Sources အချင်းချင်းတူ/မတူကိုစစ်ပါ။
  </Step>
  <Step title="Decide Next">
    Action မလုပ်ခင် ဘာတွေထပ်စစ်ရမလဲချရေးပါ။
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Research Agent Prompt

```text
Act as my cautious research agent.

Research question:
[question]

Sources:
[paste links/notes/text]

Task:
1. Summarize the answer in 5 bullets.
2. Create an evidence table:
   - Claim
   - Source
   - Evidence
   - Confidence
   - Date sensitivity
3. List what is missing or uncertain.
4. List risks if I act on this information.
5. Give me the next 3 things to verify.

Rules:
- Do not invent sources.
- If the source does not say it, mark it as unknown.
- Separate facts from interpretation.
- Keep the answer concise.
```

## Crypto Research Variant

```text
Act as a crypto DYOR research agent.

Project:
[project name]

Inputs:
[whitepaper, website notes, tokenomics, market data]

Analyze:
1. Problem and use case
2. Product status
3. Team
4. Tokenomics
5. Market/liquidity risks
6. Red flags
7. Questions before buying

Do not give buy/sell advice.
```

## Content Research Variant

```text
Research this content topic for a Myanmar audience.

Topic:
[topic]

Return:
- Core facts
- Common misconceptions
- Local examples
- 5 content angles
- Claims that need source checking
- Safe wording for uncertain claims
```

<Warning>
Research agent ကမြန်ပေမယ့် hallucination ဖြစ်နိုင်ပါတယ်။ Important facts, current product details, legal/financial claims တွေကို source နဲ့ပြန်စစ်ပါ။
</Warning>
