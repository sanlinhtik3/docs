Daily Life Agent ဆိုတာ ကိုယ့်နေ့စဉ်အလုပ်တွေကို plan လုပ်ပေး၊ draft ရေးပေး၊ decision support ပေး၊ checklist စစ်ပေးတဲ့ AI workflow ပါ။

## TL;DR

- Personal assistant လိုသုံးပါ၊ autopilot မလုပ်ပါနဲ့။
- Calendar, money, private info တွေကို AI ထဲမထည့်ခင် privacy စဉ်းစားပါ။
- Output တိုင်းကို human review လုပ်ပါ။
- Reusable daily prompts သုံးရင် time save ဖြစ်ပါတယ်။

## Morning Operator

နေ့စဉ်အစမှာ priority ရှင်းဖို့သုံးပါ။

```text
Act as my daily planning operator.

Today’s raw tasks:
[task list ထည့်ပါ]

My constraints:
- Available time: [x hours]
- Energy level: [low/medium/high]
- Must-finish items: [list]

Create:
1. Top 3 priorities
2. Time-blocked plan
3. Tasks to defer
4. One risk I should watch
5. First 15-minute action

Keep it concise.
```

## Message Reply Operator

Email/DM reply ရေးဖို့သုံးပါ။ Private data ဖယ်ပြီးမှထည့်ပါ။

```text
Act as my reply-writing assistant.

Situation:
[ဘာဖြစ်နေလဲ]

Recipient:
[friend/client/team/customer]

Tone:
[friendly/professional/direct]

Draft a reply that:
- Is clear and respectful
- Avoids over-explaining
- Includes the next action
- Does not promise anything I cannot deliver

Give me 2 versions: short and warm.
```

## Learning Operator

ဘာသာရပ်ခက်တာတွေကိုနားလည်ဖို့သုံးပါ။

```text
Act as my Burmese learning coach.

Topic:
[topic]

My level:
[beginner/intermediate]

Explain with:
1. Simple Burmese explanation
2. English key terms in parentheses
3. One Myanmar example
4. 5-question quiz
5. One practical exercise
```

## Decision Support Operator

ဆုံးဖြတ်ချက်ကို AI ဆုံးဖြတ်ခိုင်းတာမဟုတ်ဘဲ options စစ်ခိုင်းတာပါ။

```text
Act as my decision support operator.

Decision:
[ဆုံးဖြတ်ရမယ့်အရာ]

Options:
[A, B, C]

Evaluate each option by:
- Benefits
- Risks
- Cost/time
- Reversibility
- What information is missing

Do not decide for me. Give me a clear comparison and the safest next step.
```

<Warning>
Financial, medical, legal, account access, password, OTP, API key, seed phrase စတဲ့ sensitive info တွေကို AI ထဲမထည့်ပါနဲ့။
</Warning>
